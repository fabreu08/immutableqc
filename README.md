# Immutable QC (IQC)

> **Trust, Automated. The Lab Oracle.**

**Immutable QC (IQC)** is the high-integrity middleware layer that bridges the gap between physical analytical instruments and any digital ledger. We ensure your data is born compliant.

---

## 📉 The $10B+ Data Integrity Crisis

In a digital world, the "Last Mile" of laboratory data is still surprisingly manual. Every year, life science and advanced manufacturing firms lose billions to transcription errors, fragmented audit trails, and disconnected systems.

* **The Workflow Friction:** Technicians transcribe HPLC results into spreadsheets. pH readings are recorded by hand. Critical metadata is siloed across disparate software.
* **The Compliance Risk:** One keystroke error. One forgotten field. One disconnected database. And suddenly, your regulatory audit trail is compromised.
* **The Reality:** Your instruments speak digital, but your compliance process is trapped in the era of paper. Trust shouldn't depend on human memory.

---

## 🛡️ Machine-Signed Truth: The IQC Middleware

IQC is a protocol-agnostic middleware layer designed to automate the provenance of laboratory data. We remove the "human-in-the-loop" vulnerability from the data lifecycle.

Your laboratory ecosystem—HPLC, Mass Spectrometers, pH meters, and environmental sensors—connects directly to an IQC instance. As measurements occur, IQC cryptographically signs and commits the data directly to the blockchain.

### The IQC Advantage

* **Source-to-Ledger Provenance:** Data is captured and locked at the moment of creation.
* **Human-Free Transcription:** Eliminates manual entry and the risk of post-hoc data manipulation.
* **Regulatory-Ready Audit Trails:** Real-time, cryptographically verifiable documentation for **21 CFR Part 11** and **ISO** compliance.
* **Trust Orchestration:** A single, immutable source of truth for multi-site operations and external stakeholders.
